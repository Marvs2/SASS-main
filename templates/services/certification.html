Processing of Request for Certification (Grades, Bonafide Student, General Weighted Average)

Process request for Certification of Student and Alumni. The Certification is based on the records available of the respective colleges. These certification are issued for evaluation purposes and not a substitute to the Official Transcript of Record issued by the University Registar.

  Checklist of Requirements:
  • Accomplished Request Form from Respective Branch/Campus Offices.
  • Identification Card (ID).
  • If Representative:
      - Authorization Letter and Identification Card (ID) if claimant is not the owner of the document.
