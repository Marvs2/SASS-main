Application for Shifting

Process request for students who intend to shift to another program in the University. Student mus have one(1) year residency from the current program to qualify for shifting to another program. Application for shifting shall be accomplished prior to the start of the next academic school year. Qualification varies depending on the program where the student intends to shift.

  Checklist of Requirements:
  • Certified Copy of Grades at Admission Office.
  • Full-accomplished Shifting Form at Respective College/Branch/Campus.
