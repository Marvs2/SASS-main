Processing of Application for Change of Enrollment (Change of Schedule/Subjects)

Process request for application for change of enrollment specifically for change of schedule or subject/s. This is both for regular and irregular student of the University and is done during the adjustment period.

  Checklist of Requirements:
  • Accomplished and printed copy of ACE form (Change of Schedule/Subject) downloadable from www.pup.edu.ph/downloads/students/
