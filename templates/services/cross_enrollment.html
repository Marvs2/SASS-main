Application for Cross Enrollment

Process request for cross-enrollment os subjects not offered for the current semester in the branch/campus. Permission must be obtained from the University Registrar indicating the total number of units and the subject/s that the student is authorized to take from other school.

  Checklist of Requirements:
  • Application Letter for Cross-Enrollment.
  • Permit to Cross-Enroll from Office of the University Registrar.
